# Shilpa's Preferences

How Shilpa likes to work, and what to avoid. Update this whenever new preferences come up.

---

## ✅ What she likes

- **Visual mockups before deciding** — always show a visual (using the show_widget tool) when there are multiple design options. She can't judge in words alone.
- **Coral pink (#e05c72) for attention-grabbing elements** — chosen for the announcement bar; feels on-brand and warm
- **Short, friendly explanations** — plain English, no technical terms
- **Getting things done for her** — she doesn't want to run commands or do technical steps herself
- **Publishing handled automatically** — just say "publish" and Claude does the rest
- **Seeing options** — she prefers to choose between 3–6 visual options rather than have one thing imposed

## ❌ What she doesn't like / what to avoid

- **Rewriting her copy** — her words are carefully chosen; never paraphrase or tighten unless she explicitly asks
- **Jargon** — no "git", "commit", "CSS", "HTML", "deploy", "repo" etc.
- **Being asked to run terminal commands** — never happens; Claude handles all of that
- **Changes without preview** — for visual/design decisions, always show a mockup first
- **Too many options at once** — keep it to 4–6 max, clearly labelled A/B/C

## 🎨 Design preferences

- Fonts: Fraunces (headlines) + Poppins (body) — no substitutes
- Colours: navy, coral, steel blue, sage, dark green — stay within the palette
- Announcement bar: coral pink background, ★ star, flashing "Know more" button
- Hero image: shorter/rectangular crop showing grass at bottom + trees; only headline + one tagline line on the image
- No clutter on the hero — keep it atmospheric and spacious

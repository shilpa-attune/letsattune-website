Know Your Coach

Shilpa Jahagirdar

Founder · ICF Certified Coach · IT Professional

I am a PCC-certified Coach accredited by the International Coaching Federation (ICF), with a background in IT and over 10 years of experience in Corporate Training and Leadership Development.

While my professional journey began in the corporate world, I found myself increasingly drawn towards understanding people, emotions, behaviour, and what truly helps us navigate life's challenges. That curiosity eventually led me to coaching - and it felt like coming home.

That's when, 6 years ago, I founded Lets Attune with a simple intention - to create reflective and emotionally safe spaces where people can pause, understand themselves more deeply, and navigate life's challenges with greater clarity and awareness.

Today, through coaching, workshops, listening circles, and group programs, I support individuals navigating transitions, uncertainty, emotional overwhelm, and personal growth, helping them reconnect with themselves and move forward with greater confidence and intention.

Beyond Coaching

Alongside my coaching journey, I have explored and studied areas such as Shadow Work, Transactional Analysis (TA), Nonviolent Communication (NVC), the Enneagram, and Psychology.

Over the years, I have facilitated workshops, supported individuals through periods of uncertainty and transition, and contributed to emotional wellbeing initiatives, including work with an NGO in the areas of mental distress and suicide prevention.

Apart from coaching, I am also drawn to energy healing, spirituality, and deeper inner exploration. I believe that growth can happen through awareness, reflection, conversation, and sometimes simply through creating space to listen more deeply to ourselves.

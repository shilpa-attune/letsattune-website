Who is this for?

This workshop is for women who are ready to pause, reconnect with themselves, and embrace the next chapter of their lives with greater clarity and purpose.

Are you one or more of these?

  • Wondering where you fit in amidst the many roles and responsibilities you carry?
  • "What do I want for myself now?" after having spent years putting your work, children and family first.
  • Feeling emotionally tired, even though life appears normal for others?
  • Constantly managing responsibilities while realising that there has been very little time or space for yourself.
  • Standing at a crossroads in life, unsure of what comes next, struggling to make a decision, or finding it difficult to move forward?

Wouldn't you want to instead?

  • Feel like yourself again—confident, capable, and connected to who you truly are.
  • Create space for your own needs, dreams, and aspirations without feeling guilty about it.
  • Trust yourself and your choices, even when life feels uncertain or demanding.
  • Feel balanced and intentional in how you approach your responsibilities, relationships, and future.
  • Look ahead with greater clarity, confidence, and excitement for the life you want to create.

What we'll explore together

What am I feeling, and what is it trying to tell me?

What have my experiences, choices, and responsibilities enriched me with?

What do I need in this phase of my life and what do I need to address?

And finally... What is one small but meaningful step I am ready to take?

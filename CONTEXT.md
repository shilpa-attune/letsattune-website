# Let's Attune — Brand & Content Context

The marketing website for **Let's Attune**, Shilpa Jahagirdar's coaching practice.
This file locks the brand vocabulary so site copy uses the founder's real terms — not
invented synonyms. (The earlier demo invented values like "Inclusivity/Holistic" that
do not exist in the brand.)

## ⚠️ Copy is sacred

**Use Shilpa's existing website copy VERBATIM. Do NOT tighten, paraphrase, shorten, or rewrite it.**
Shilpa has crafted the wording deliberately. The source of truth is the live Google Sites text at
`https://www.letsattune.com/`. Headings/eyebrows/UI microcopy may be added, but all body/hero/section
prose must match the live site word-for-word. (Supersedes the earlier "lightly tighten" decision.)

## Language

### Offerings (the four "Ways We Support")
**Coaching**:
One-on-one confidential conversations for navigating challenges, decisions, transitions, and growth.
_Avoid_: therapy, counselling, mentoring.

**Workshops**:
Structured group experiences for growth, awareness, reflection, and change.

**Listening Circles**:
Facilitated group spaces for shared reflection and connection — "emptying out" before solutions.
_Avoid_: support group, group therapy.

**Corporate Programs**:
Behavioural skill training, leadership development, and people-focused initiatives for organisations.
_Avoid_: B2B, enterprise.

### The approach (four steps, in order)
**Understand → Explore → Align → Integrate**:
The signature four-step coaching arc. Always this order, always these four words.
_Avoid_: reordering, renaming, or adding steps.

### Brand attributes (from the palette card)
**Explorative · Collaborative · Empathy · Motivated · Clarity**:
The five descriptive attributes printed on the brand palette. Use as brand adjectives.
_Avoid_: presenting these as the same thing as the four-step approach.

### Motto
**EXPRESS : EXPLORE : EMPOWER : EVOLVE**:
The footer tagline. Always uppercase, colon-separated, four words in this order.

### People & credentials
**Shilpa Jahagirdar**:
Founder and coach. **PCC Coach, ICF-certified.** ~10 yrs corporate training background; IT origin.
Founded Let's Attune ~6 years ago.
_Avoid_: inventing degrees, dates, or credentials beyond PCC/ICF.

**Beyond Coaching modalities**:
Shadow Work, Transactional Analysis (TA), Nonviolent Communication (NVC), Enneagram, Psychology.
Also: NGO work in mental distress & suicide prevention; energy healing & spirituality.

### Audiences ("Who we work with")
Working Professionals · Managers & Leaders · Students & Young Adults · Parents and Women ·
Business Owners & Entrepreneurs · Individuals Navigating Change.

## Relationships

- **Let's Attune** delivers four **Offerings**: Coaching, Workshops, Listening Circles, Corporate Programs.
- Every coaching engagement follows the **Understand → Explore → Align → Integrate** arc.
- **Shilpa** is the sole **Coach** and **Founder**.
- A **Workshop** is a time-bound, dated event (e.g. the Women's Reset & Reconnection Workshop).

A PCC Coach certified by ICF, Shilpa comes with an IT background and over 10 years of experience in Corporate Training and Leadership Development. She is trained in Shadow Work, Emotional Intelligence, Positive Psychology, and is an NLP Practitioner.

Once she began her coach training, life made more sense. She went deeper into the world of psychology and realised this was her true purpose — to just be there for people. To be a listening ear, without advice, without judgement. Be it a simple one-day incident or a complex long-term plan, it's about figuring out what is happening within and what you are really looking for.

Her specialty is helping people identify and achieve personal goals by coping with constant worry, overthinking, distress, anxiety, or stress. She also does extensive work with an NGO in areas of mental distress and suicide prevention, and has an inclination towards energy healing and spirituality.
